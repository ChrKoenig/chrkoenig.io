About me

I’m a biodiversity data scientist and ecological modeler who seeks new challenges outside academia. My technical interests range from making engaging visualization to implementing predictive machine learning workflows, from dabbling with interesting datasets to building robust backends and server environments. In my free time I am passionate about martial arts, photography and all kinds of guitar-driven Rock music.
